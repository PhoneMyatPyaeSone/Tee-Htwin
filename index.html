Tee Htwin For Youths

Tee Htwin is a non profit organization based in United States which focus on providing STEAM education to the underrepresented Students in Myanmar.

Get To Know Us

We are a team made of High School students and University Students who are interested in creating and sharing for learning new fields related to the STEAM.We have leaders around the country to organize, persuade the teenagers who are between 12 - 20.

Our Mission

Meeting#01

Our main focus is to educate teachers, students, and educators in STEAM Field to create a better learning environment. We also want to create STEAM events, competitions and webniars to help High School students who are confused to choose their career.

Meeting#02

We want to help the underrepresented students in both academic and extracurricular activities. By the end of 2022, we expect that we will educate 10000 students and 1000 hours of lectures.

Meeting#03

Internet Data charges in Myanmar have hugely impacted the mid-class family which is now equal to 1 day's salary of a normal employee. Citizens in Myanmar get only 4 hours of electricity for the whole day which makes online learning more difficult for students. Students are in danger to attend in-person school because there aren't any legal schools open for in-person.
